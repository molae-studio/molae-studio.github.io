About

MOLAE-STUDIO is more than 20 years of 3d assets creation for games, 3d renders and scripting. Passionate, we started using Autodesk and Adobe (Macromedia) products in the 90', creating content for games like Half-life and serious-sam. We later made mods for FarCry, Crysis and Battlefield, product visualization and mobile apps.

The company is based between France and South-Korea, feel free to contact us to say hello!
